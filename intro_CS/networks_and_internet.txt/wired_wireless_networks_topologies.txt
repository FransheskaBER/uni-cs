LAN VS WAN (local area network vs wide are network)
- LAN -> computers in one physical location
- WAN -> LANs connected over large distances like between Madrid and London
- INTERNET -> the largest WAN (millions of LANs connected worldwide)


NETWORK DEVICES (hubs, switches, routers)
- hub doesnt have intelligence. It receives the signal and send it out to all port with no filter.
- switch has basic intelligence. It reads the hardware addressses, filters the broadcast traffic 
    and prevent broadcast storms.
- routers has the most intelligence. It reads the IP addresses and sends the packets only to the
    right destinations.

- Wire networks consider to be safer than wireless, because wifi the signal is broadcasted
    through the air and anyone within reach can access it (like hub that broadcast to all ports) 
    so thats why wifi encryption like WPA2 or WPA3 is critical for security reasons.

WIRED VS WIRELESS TRADEOFF
Aspect              Wired                       Wireless
Mobility            Limited by cable            Free movement within range
Troubleshooting     Easier (follow the cable)   Harder (invisible signals)
Speed               Fastest                     Medium (catching up)
Security            Much more secure            Easy to intercept signal
Setup cost          Must buy/run cables         Just need access point
